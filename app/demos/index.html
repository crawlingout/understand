Other resources

Audiobooks - you can buy them or even get some of them for free at sites like LibriVox (they have books in English and many other languages) or Open Culture (where you can download epic Foundation Trilogy by Isaac Asimov, the complete Chronicles of Narnia by C.S. Lewis and many other books).

Podcasts - This American Life (English) or Radio Ambulante (Spanish) are great examples. Podcasts often have transcripts. Some exceptionally amazing podcasts are so good that just being able to listen to them is good enough reason to learn the language.
