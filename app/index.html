How to use this app?

You will be flexing your language-learning brain muscles with an interesting audiobook (you need both audio + text).

1 Start listening without reading the text. Try to catch as much as you can. When you get lost, pause the audio.

2 Read the part you just listened to. Figure out the incomprehensible parts. Highlight words to have them translated.

3 Listen to the same part again. If you now understand comfortably, enjoy it and continue to the next part.

For more details, read the article: How I Rewired My Brain to Really Understand a Foreign Language.
