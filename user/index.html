Quota for translations

This web application has to use a paid 3rd party service to translate words. That's why I had to set up some limits.
Currently, the limit is 4000 characters per month.

To be honest, I have not yet figured out the best way to finance it. Ads? Paid premium accounts with statistics and exporting your vocabulary to flashcards?

Anyway, if you have run out of your quota for translations, please shoot me an email at jan at icanunderstand dot eu and I will increase your quota. It's for free, at least while this app is still in beta.
