Dear language learner,

I know surprisingly many people who have been studying grammar and vocabulary for years and still struggle to understand a conversation or watch a movie without subtitles.

I think a lot of them confuse learning a language with learning about a language. Trying to learn a language by memorizing grammar rules is like trying to learn to play football by memorizing tactical variants. Some things cannot be learned from textbooks.

For example the ability to understand spoken word - you need to learn to recognize words instantly and understand their meaning without translating in your head. The only way to acquire this skill is through practice.

The purpose of this app is to help you practice with authentic audio content. In my experience it's a lot more efficient than traditional textbook learning... and it's fun.
